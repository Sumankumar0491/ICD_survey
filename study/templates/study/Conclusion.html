The judge, who reviewed any appeals, was told about the payment scheme for each player and received a flat rate payment for his/her work. This experiment parallels situations that one may encounter in life when the advisor has a financial interest in the advice that they give, but the advisee can take action if they feel they have been treated unfairly.
