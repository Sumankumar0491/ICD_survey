You will be rewarded for the accuracy of your estimate.

If you estimate is within 10 dots of the correct number, you will receive a {{ Constants.estimator_bonus }} bonus.

If your estimate is off by more than 10, you will receive no bonus.

For example, if the correct number of solid dots is 450, you will earn a {{ Constants.estimator_bonus }} bonus if you estimate a number between 440-460. If you estimate 439 or lower, or 461 or higher, you will receive nothing.
